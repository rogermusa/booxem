The Universal Law of Attraction

This book presents the core teachings of "Abraham," a group of non-physical entities channeled by Esther Hicks. The central tenet is the Law of Attraction: that which is like unto itself is drawn. Put simply, you attract into your life whatever you give your attention, energy, and focus to, whether wanted or unwanted.

Everything in the Universe, including your thoughts and emotions, vibrates at a certain frequency. According to Abraham-Hicks, you are constantly broadcasting a vibrational signal based on your predominant thoughts and feelings. The Law of Attraction unerringly matches that signal, bringing you circumstances, people, and experiences of a similar vibration.

Therefore, the key to creating a joyful and fulfilling life is to consciously manage your own vibration by deliberately choosing thoughts and focusing on feelings that align with what you desire.

"That which is like unto itself, is drawn. ... You are a Vibrational Transmitter, and you are broadcasting a signal in every moment of your existence."

— Abraham-Hicks

Fundamental Principles of Deliberate Creation

The teachings of Abraham emphasize several core principles for understanding and applying the Law of Attraction:

You Are a Vibrational Being

You exist fundamentally as energy, constantly vibrating. Your thoughts and emotions dictate the frequency of this vibration.

The Stream of Well-Being

The Universe is fundamentally based on Well-Being. This stream flows constantly to you, unless you pinch it off with resistant (negative) thoughts and vibrations.

Your Emotional Guidance System

Your emotions are indicators of your vibrational alignment with your desires and the Stream of Well-Being. Feeling good means you're aligned; feeling bad means you're offering resistance.

The Power of Focus

What you focus on expands. Giving attention to something includes it in your vibration, whether you want it or not. Deliberately focus on what you *do* want.

The Art of Allowing

Allowing means releasing resistance to Well-Being and your desires. It's about achieving vibrational alignment with what you want by feeling good *now*.

Your Vibrational Vortex

Every desire you've ever launched creates a vibrational reality (the "Vortex") where it already exists. Your work is to align your current vibration with the vibration of your Vortex.

The Three Steps to Deliberate Creation

Abraham-Hicks presents a three-step process for deliberately creating your reality:

  • 1
    Step 1: You Ask.

    Contrast helps you identify your desires. Every time you experience something unwanted, a desire for its opposite is automatically launched vibrationally. This step happens naturally.

  • 2
    Step 2: The Universe Answers.

    Source Energy / The Universe immediately answers your vibrational request. Your desire exists in your Vortex. This is not your work; it's the Universe's job.

  • 3
    Step 3: You Allow It In (Receive).

    This is *your* crucial work: achieving vibrational alignment with your desire so you can receive it. This means finding ways to feel good, release resistance, and match the frequency of what you've asked for.

"The entire Universe is conspiring to give you everything that you want."

— Abraham-Hicks

Tools for Raising Your Vibration

The teachings offer various "processes" to help shift focus and improve emotional state (vibration):

  • Segment Intending: Before entering a new segment of your day (a meeting, a drive, a conversation), briefly set your intention for how you want to feel and what you want to accomplish.
  • The Process of Pivoting: When you notice unwanted thoughts or feelings, gently pivot your focus towards what you *do* want.
  • Rampage of Appreciation: Deliberately focus on things you appreciate, building momentum of positive feeling.
  • Understanding the Emotional Scale: Recognize that moving from despair to anger is vibrational progress. Aim to gradually move up the emotional scale towards joy and appreciation.
  • Getting into the Receiving Mode: Practices like meditation, spending time in nature, or listening to music can help quiet resistant thought and allow receiving.

Becoming a Deliberate Creator

"The Law of Attraction," as presented by Esther and Jerry Hicks through the teachings of Abraham, offers a framework for understanding reality based on vibration and focus. It posits that you are the powerful creator of your own experience, attracting circumstances that match your dominant thoughts and feelings.

By learning to manage your focus, guide your emotions towards feeling good, and release resistance, you can align yourself with the ever-present Stream of Well-Being and deliberately attract the joy, abundance, and experiences you desire. The key lies in mastering the Art of Allowing through conscious vibrational management.

Focus your thoughts, tend to your feelings, and align with your desires. You hold the vibrational key to creating the life you choose. Feel good now, and allow your well-being to flow.
