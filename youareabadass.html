Core Badass Principles

Sincero lays out several key principles to help you shift your mindset and start living awesomely:

Your Thoughts Create Your Reality

Understand that your subconscious beliefs shape your experiences. Identify limiting beliefs (often formed in childhood) and consciously choose new, empowering thoughts. What you focus on expands.

Tap into Source Energy

Recognize that there's a universal energy available to you. Connect with it through gratitude, meditation, being present, and aligning your desires with this higher frequency. Faith is key.

Love Yourself Like Crazy

Self-love is not selfish; it's fundamental. Appreciate your uniqueness, forgive yourself for past mistakes, and treat yourself with kindness and respect. Stop apologizing for who you are.

Get Clear on What You Want

Define your desires clearly and specifically. Vague wishes get vague results. Understand *why* you want it â connect emotion to your goals. Make a **decision** to go after it.

Take Action (Even Messy Action)

Don't wait for perfect conditions. Overcome fear and procrastination by taking **imperfect, decisive action** towards your goals. Learn and adjust as you go. Step outside your comfort zone.

Fix Your Money Mindset

Address limiting beliefs about money. See wealth as energy and a tool for good. Respect money, be grateful for what you have, and believe in your ability to earn more by providing value.

Badass Moves: Taking Action

The book is packed with exercises. Key actions encouraged include:

  • Use Affirmations: Create and repeat positive statements about yourself and your goals as if they are already true.
  • Practice Visualization: Regularly imagine yourself having already achieved your desires, feeling the emotions associated with success.
  • Cultivate Gratitude: Make a habit of noticing and appreciating the good things in your life right now. Gratitude raises your frequency.
  • Meditate: Quiet the mental chatter and connect with your inner self and Source Energy. Even short sessions help.
  • Practice Forgiveness: Release resentment towards others and yourself. Holding onto anger drains your energy and blocks abundance.
  • Make a Decision: Commit fully to your goals. Indecision keeps you stuck. Decide you're going for it, no matter what.
  • Surround Yourself with Support: Find mentors, coaches, or friends who believe in you and lift you higher. Ditch the Debbie Downers.
